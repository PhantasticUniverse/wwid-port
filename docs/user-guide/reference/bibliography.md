# Bibliography

[User Guide](../index.md) > [Reference] > Bibliography

Published sources for the acoustic models, optimization algorithms, and physical property calculations used in WIDesigner.

## Acoustics and Musical Instruments

1. Neville H. Fletcher and Thomas D. Rossing, *The Physics of Musical Instruments*, 2nd edition, Springer, 2010.

2. Antoine Chaigne and Jean Kergomard, *Acoustique des instruments de musique* (Acoustics of Musical Instruments), 2nd edition, Belin, 2013.

## Tonehole Modeling

3. Antoine Lefebvre and Gary P. Scavone, "Characterization of woodwind instrument toneholes with the finite element method," *Journal of the Acoustical Society of America*, 131:3153--3163, 2012.

4. Antoine Lefebvre, "Computational Acoustic Methods for the Design of Woodwind Instruments," PhD thesis, McGill University, 2011.

5. Antoine Lefebvre and Gary P. Scavone, "Refinements to the Transfer Matrix Model of Woodwind Tone Holes," in *Proceedings of the International Symposium on Musical Acoustics* (ISMA), 2010.

## Loop Gain and Playing Ranges

6. R. Auvray, B. Fabre, and P.-Y. Lagree, "Regime change and oscillation thresholds in recorder-like instruments," *Journal of the Acoustical Society of America*, 131(2), 2012.

7. Patricio de la Cuadra, "The sound of oscillating air jets: Physics, modeling and simulation in flute-like instruments," PhD thesis, Stanford University, 2005.

## Optimization Methods

8. D. R. Jones, C. D. Perttunen, and B. E. Stuckmann, "Lipschitzian optimization without the Lipschitz constant," *Journal of Optimization Theory and Applications*, 79:157--181, 1993.

9. Steven G. Johnson, "The NLopt nonlinear-optimization package," http://github.com/stevengj/nlopt.

10. Steven G. Johnson, "cdirect re-implementation of DIRECT and DIRECT-L algorithms," 2014.

11. Arash Tavassoli, Kambiz Haji Hajikolaei, Saber DorMohammadi, and G. Gary Wang, "Modification of DIRECT for high-dimensional design problems," *Engineering Optimization*, 2013.

## Air Properties

12. P. T. Tsilingiris, "Thermophysical and transport properties of humid air at temperature range between 0 and 100 C," *Energy Conversion and Management*, 49:1098--1110, 2008.

13. A. Picard, R. S. Davis, M. Glaser, and K. Fujii, "Revised formula for the density of moist air (CIPM-2007)," *Metrologia*, 45:149--155, 2008.

14. M. A. McQuillan, J. M. Culham, and M. M. Yovanovich, "Properties of Dry Air at One Atmosphere," Microelectronics Heat Transfer Lab Report UW/MHTL 8406 G-01, University of Waterloo, 1984.
